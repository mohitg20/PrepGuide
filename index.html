    Prep Guide for JEE Main + Advanced is a non profit initiative by me,with a vision to help JEE aspirants and provide personal mentorship which can guide them to clear all hurdles that come in the way of this preperation.

  • How is Prep Guide different from other mentorship and coaching platforms?

    I myself have given JEE mains and Advanced last year and thus I know the latest trend of JEE exams, i.e., To take max output of min but directional effort. So I can understand and connect with you better on personal level. Normally mentors on other platforms are either not competent enough or earlier IITians which teach according to the past curriculum and also demand heavy fees.

    Prep Guide is a FREE coaching organisation. Regular study materials, guidance sessions, preperation tips, guide books, and test papers will be provided on regular basis.

    You don't need any more material. My resources are handpicked from the best content available and which is much more concise than what I myself used for preperation. These will be sufficient enough to clear the JEE Advanced exam if you give your ful effort.
